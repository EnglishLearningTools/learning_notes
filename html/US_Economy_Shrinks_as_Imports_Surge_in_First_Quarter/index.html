【美国第一季度经济萎缩，进口激增】

Summary: The US economy contracted due to surging imports and tariff distortions, with weak consumer spending but strong business investment. Equipment imports surged ahead of tariffs, inflating inventories. Government spending fell, and wage growth slowed, reflecting budget cuts.

摘要： 美国经济因进口激增和关税扭曲而收缩，消费者支出疲软但企业投资强劲。设备进口在关税前激增，推高了库存。政府支出下降，工资增长放缓，反映出预算削减。
