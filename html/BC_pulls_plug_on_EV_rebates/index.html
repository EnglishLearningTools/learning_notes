B.C. pulls plug on EV rebates

【卑诗省取消电动汽车补贴】

Summary: British Columbia is ending its EV rebate program on May 15th, leaving buyers with only two weeks to claim up to $4,000. The federal rebate program also ran out earlier this year, removing a combined $9,000 discount. Dealers worry the end of rebates, combined with strict ZEV mandates, will burden consumers.

摘要： 卑诗省将于5月15日终止电动汽车补贴计划，买家仅剩两周时间申请最高4000加元的补贴。联邦补贴计划今年早些也已耗尽，两项补贴合计可为电动汽车减免9000加元。经销商担忧补贴终止与严格的零排放汽车强制令叠加将加重消费者负担。
