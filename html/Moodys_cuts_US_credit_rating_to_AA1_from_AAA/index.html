Moody's cuts U.S. credit rating to AA1 from AAA

【穆迪将美国信用评级从AAA下调至AA1】

Summary: Moody's downgrades U.S. credit rating to AA from AAA due to rising debt and interest payments, but improves the outlook to stable, expecting debt to reach 134% of GDP by 2035.

摘要： 穆迪因债务和利息支付增加将美国信用评级从AAA下调至AA，但将展望调整为稳定，预计到2035年债务将达到GDP的134%。
