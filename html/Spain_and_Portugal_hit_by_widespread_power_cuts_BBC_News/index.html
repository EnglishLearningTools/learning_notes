Spain and Portugal hit by widespread power cuts | BBC News

【西班牙和葡萄牙遭遇大面积停电 | BBC新闻】

Summary: A massive power cut has affected large parts of Spain and Portugal, disrupting public transport, communications, and businesses, though hospitals remain operational with generators. The cause is unknown, but governments are holding emergency meetings, and restoring power may take 6-10 hours. Chaos is reported, especially in transport, with metro systems and traffic lights down. The outage also mildly affects parts of France. Authorities are coordinating responses, and the BBC is monitoring developments.
